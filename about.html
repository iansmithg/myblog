A little background about me...

A graduate of The Ohio State University.

A Buckeye for life.

An obsessive learner...I love picking up new skills and spending time perfecting my craft. If you know me, you know I am always talking about the next skill I plan on teaching myself.

Addicted to success...I never lose sight of my vision.

A lover of creation and innovation.

A starter and a grower.

I enjoy thinking of how to do things differently.

In the summer of 2014 I watched the Jobs movie over a dozen times. In that movie was a quote changed my life forever:

When you grow up you tend to get told the world is the way that it is...that your life is just to live your life inside the world, try not to bash into the walls too much ...but that's a very limited life, life can be much broader...once you discover one simple fact and that is that everything around you that you call life was made up by people who were no smarter than you...and you can change it, you can influence it, you can build your own things that other people can use. It's to shake off this erroneous notion that life is just there and you're just going to live it versus embrace it, change it, improve it, make your mark upon it. And once you learn that, you'll never be the same. -Steve Jobs

If you are someone who has ever felt a nudge to go against the current of the world, don't ignore that feeling. Harness it and let it catapult you forward. At times it may get tough but when you reflect back you will be glad you did.
